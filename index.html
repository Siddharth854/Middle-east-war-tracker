// Methodology & Sources

Running estimate uses a three-phase bottom-up cost model: ~$380,000,000/day for initial strikes (Days 0–3), ~$220,000,000/day for sustained operations (Days 3–10), ~$155,000,000/day for air dominance/ISR-heavy phase (Day 10+). Each phase is built from seven sourced components.

Not included: Long-term veteran healthcare (historically 2–4× direct war costs over decades), economic opportunity costs, indirect costs from energy market disruption, allied nation expenditures, or environmental remediation.
